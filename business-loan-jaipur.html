FAQ

Common Questions about Business Loan

What types of business loans are available in Jaipur?

Unsecured term loans, secured business loans, working capital / OD facilities, MSME loans, and machinery finance. Programs vary by lender — some require ITR, others accept GST or bank statement surrogate for eligible profiles.

Can I get a business loan without ITR in Jaipur?

Yes. Several lenders offer business loans on banking surrogate (average bank balance-based) or GST surrogate programs for eligible profiles without formal ITR filing. Loan amount and eligibility depend on turnover, banking history and property (if secured).

What is the minimum turnover required for a business loan?

Requirements vary by lender and loan type. Unsecured programs typically need 1–3 years of business vintage and minimum monthly banking turnover. WealthMetre AI identifies programs that match your specific business profile.

Is collateral required for a business loan?

Unsecured business loans are available for eligible profiles without collateral, though amounts may be lower. Secured programs (against property) offer higher amounts. WealthMetre identifies the right type based on your profile and requirement.

How fast can a business loan be disbursed in Jaipur?

Processing speed varies by lender and profile type. Straightforward unsecured profiles with clean banking can move faster than secured cases requiring property valuation. WealthMetre coordinates with lenders to expedite eligible cases.

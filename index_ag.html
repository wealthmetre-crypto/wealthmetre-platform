FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers on home loan, LAP, and business loan across Jaipur & Rajasthan.

Which bank gives the best home loan in Jaipur?

The best lender depends on your CIBIL score, income type, and property title. WealthMetre AI instantly compares all 140+ lenders for your exact profile across Jaipur and Rajasthan to find the most suitable options.

Can I get a loan on JDA or Society Patta property in Jaipur?

Yes. Several banks and NBFCs accept JDA-approved and Society Patta properties in Jaipur. Acceptance varies by lender and depends on specific property details. WealthMetre AI matches your property title to lenders with appropriate programs. Learn more →

What is the current LAP interest rate in Jaipur?

LAP rates vary based on CIBIL score, income type, property type and LTV ratio. WealthMetre identifies the most competitive options across 40+ LAP lenders for your specific profile and eligibility.

What CIBIL score is required for a home loan?

Most banks prefer CIBIL 700+. Several NBFCs consider profiles from 600 depending on income and property value. WealthMetre AI shows all lenders who may approve your exact CIBIL score. Low CIBIL loan options →

How much loan can I get against my property?

Typically 50–70% of property value for residential, 50–65% for commercial, 50–60% for industrial properties. Final LTV depends on income, FOIR, CIBIL and lender policy.

Can a self-employed person get a business loan without ITR?

Yes. Several lenders offer business loans on banking surrogate or GST surrogate for eligible self-employed profiles. Availability and amount depend on banking history. Business loan options →

What documents are required for a home loan?

PAN, Aadhaar, 3 months salary slips, Form 16, 6 months bank statements, property documents. Self-employed: ITR for 2 years, P&L, balance sheet, GST returns. Our advisors provide a tailored checklist for your specific lender.

Does WealthMetre serve cities outside Jaipur in Rajasthan?

Yes. We serve clients across all major Rajasthan cities including Jodhpur, Udaipur, Kota, Ajmer, Bhilwara, Alwar, Sikar, Bikaner and others. Our AI platform works pan-Rajasthan with remote advisory support.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers on home loan, LAP, and business loan across Jaipur & Rajasthan.

Which bank gives the best home loan in Jaipur?

The best lender depends on your CIBIL score, income type, and property title. WealthMetre AI instantly compares all 140+ lenders for your exact profile.

Can I get a loan on JDA or Society Patta property in Jaipur?

Yes. Several banks and NBFCs accept JDA-approved and Society Patta properties. Learn more →

What is the current LAP interest rate in Jaipur?

LAP rates vary based on CIBIL score, income type, property type and LTV ratio. WealthMetre identifies the most competitive options across 40+ LAP lenders.

What CIBIL score is required for a home loan?

Most banks prefer CIBIL 700+. Several NBFCs consider profiles from 600. Low CIBIL options →

How much loan can I get against my property?

Typically 50–70% of property value for residential, 50–65% for commercial. Final LTV depends on income, FOIR, CIBIL and lender policy.

Can a self-employed person get a business loan without ITR?

Yes. Several lenders offer business loans on banking or GST surrogate. Business loan options →

What documents are required for a home loan?

PAN, Aadhaar, 3 months salary slips, Form 16, 6 months bank statements, property documents. Self-employed: ITR 2 years, P&L, balance sheet, GST returns.

Does WealthMetre serve cities outside Jaipur?

Yes. We serve Jodhpur, Udaipur, Kota, Ajmer, Bhilwara, Alwar, Sikar, Bikaner and all Rajasthan cities with remote advisory support.

Common Questions about Home Loan / LAP

Which lenders give home loan on JDA-approved property in Jaipur?

Multiple banks and NBFCs accept JDA-approved properties in Jaipur. The specific lenders depend on JDA scheme number, property location, valuation and your profile. WealthMetre AI matches your JDA property details to eligible programs.

Can I get loan on Society Patta property in Jaipur?

Yes. Several NBFCs and housing finance companies offer loans on Society Patta properties in Jaipur, subject to property specifics and eligibility criteria. Lender acceptance depends on the specific society and profile details.

What is the difference between JDA and Registry property for loans?

Registry (registered title) typically has the widest lender acceptance. JDA-approved properties have good acceptance with many lenders. Society Patta and Panchayati Patta have more limited acceptance, concentrated among NBFCs and housing finance companies.

Can I get LAP on Panchayati Patta property?

Yes, for eligible profiles. Several housing finance companies and NBFCs accept Panchayati and Gram Panchayat properties, typically in rural/semi-urban Jaipur areas. Eligibility conditions on valuation, location and income apply.

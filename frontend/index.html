FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about loans, eligibility, and WealthMetre's advisory service.

Which bank gives best home loan in Jaipur?
The best lender depends on your CIBIL score, income type, property title and loan amount. WealthMetre AI compares 140+ banks and NBFCs to find the most suitable program for your exact profile — instantly and for free.
Can I get a loan on JDA or Society Patta property?
Yes. Several banks and NBFCs in our network accept JDA-approved and Society Patta properties in Jaipur and across Rajasthan. Eligibility depends on property specifics and your credit profile. WealthMetre AI matches your property title to the right lenders automatically.
What CIBIL score is needed for a home loan?
Most banks prefer CIBIL 700+. However, several NBFCs in our network consider profiles from CIBIL 600 depending on income, property value and loan amount. WealthMetre AI shows you which lenders are likely to approve your exact score.
Can I get a business loan without ITR?
Yes. Several lenders offer business loans on banking surrogate or GST surrogate for eligible self-employed profiles. Availability and loan amount depend on banking history and business vintage. WealthMetre identifies the right surrogate program for you.
Is WealthMetre's service completely free?
Yes — 100% free for borrowers. WealthMetre earns a referral commission from banks and NBFCs upon successful loan disbursal. You pay nothing extra — the interest rate you get is the same as approaching the bank directly.
Which cities does WealthMetre serve?
We serve all major Rajasthan cities — Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Kota, Ajmer, Bhilwara, Alwar, Bikaner, Sikar, Pali, Barmer, Sri Ganganagar, Chittorgarh, Nagaur, Tonk and more. Physical office in Jaipur at Anchor Mall, Civil Lines.
